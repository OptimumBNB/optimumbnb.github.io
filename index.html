About Us

We are international seasoned entrepreneurs, made up of engineers from market finance, data science and web development. We are seeking to develop a disruptive technological solution to optimize the real estate market in Portugal and Europe.

We are hiring:

We are looking for someone who embodies an entrepreneurial spirit, pays strong attention to detail and wants to be part of the next big thing. We are right now looking for a frontend or full stack developer and web designer.

What we offer

  • Being part of an entrepreunerial team. You will be able to work in any project and share with smart individuals.
  • No fixed schedule, be smart and efficient and you won’t have to stay longer.
  • Competitive pay based on your experience.
  • We are located in Lisbon downtown : Chiado.

Opportunities

Frontend / Full Stack Developer

  • Javascript expert. Coffeescript familiarity is a plus.
  • Experience with Javascript Frameworks (AngularJS, ReactJS, RactiveJS, MeteorJS, etc).
  • Familiar with NodeJS.
  • Git proficiency is valued, and an updated GitHub account even more.
  • Strong CSS3 skills (LESS or SASS are a plus).
  • Additional experience with functional programming languages like Haskell, Scala, Erlang, etc are a plus, although not required.
  • Team player, quickly adaptable to a startup environment.

Send us your CV to jobs@optimumbnb.com

Web Designer

  • Understands web design, usability and analytics.
  • Knows HTML5 and CSS3 really well, works with all browsers.
  • LESS or SASS knowledge is a plus.
  • Team player, quickly adaptable to a startup environment.
